# Norwegian 4x4 — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 28, 2026
**Developer:** Alice Winthrop

## Overview

Norwegian 4x4 is a workout timer app for iPhone and Apple Watch. We take a privacy-first approach: the app works on your device, and your data is not sent to us or to anyone else. Any feature that shares your data with another service will always be something you choose to turn on.

## Data the App Accesses

With your explicit permission, Norwegian 4x4 reads and writes the following via Apple HealthKit:

* Heart rate samples
* Workout sessions and summaries

This data is stored in Apple Health on your device. The app does not maintain a separate database or copy of your health data.

## Data Collection

Norwegian 4x4 does **not** collect or transmit your personal data. As of this version:

* No analytics or crash-reporting tools are included.
* No advertising frameworks are included.
* The app does not send your data to any server, third party, or the developer.
* No account is required to use the app.

## HealthKit Data

Health data accessed through HealthKit is used solely to display workout metrics to you in real time and to save completed workouts to Apple Health.

Your health data is **never** sold or used for advertising or marketing. It is used only to provide app features to you, and it is never shared with anyone without your explicit action.

## Tracking

Norwegian 4x4 does not track you across apps or websites. The app does not use the App Tracking Transparency framework because there is nothing to track.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that process user data.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.
